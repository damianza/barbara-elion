About

Barbara is the author of one of South Africa’s bestselling titles on intercultural interaction and diversity, Clues to Culture, as well as fable, The Wisdom of Hiss.

More than 50 000 copies have been sold in South Africa and the title continues to be the go-to reference for schools, the hospitality industry, hospices, corporate business and the general public. It is published and sold by One Life Media cc.

This quirky Afro-cosmic novella, written as a fable, has been sold to schools in South Africa and the United Kingdom. It has been written for enjoyment by listening audiences, and published, in a limited edition, for those who appreciate high production value. The story can be enjoyed by old and young alike. The title is available from One Life Media cc.
